About

I'm a web developer and illustrator from Barcelona. I specialize in illustration portfolios, corporate and educational sites, and small online stores. I have developed sites for cultural, artistic and educational projects.

I like working with design teams, and I can also manage projects from scratch, contributing knowledge from other areas such as graphic design, illustration, animation or music. I do enjoy new challenges.
